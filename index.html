FAQ

Answers for new members.

Is Onevia insurance?

No. Onevia is a membership-based clinic. You can pair membership with a high-deductible plan for major events.

What does membership include?

All primary care visits, preventive care, chronic disease management, telemedicine, and direct access.

How quickly can I be seen?

Members typically receive same- or next-day appointments with extended visit times.

Do you offer employer plans?

Yes. Employers can provide predictable care access with transparent, per-member pricing.

Are pharmacy, dental, and vision available?

Primary care launches first. Pharmacy, dental, and vision are planned expansions on the same membership model.
