Why Onevia

The care model that rewards health, not volume.

Traditional healthcare is driven by billing codes, short visits, and fragmented handoffs. Onevia flips the incentives. Membership revenue enables small panels, extended appointments, and proactive care, so the best financial outcome is also the best clinical outcome.

You get time with your physician, a direct line to your care team, and transparent pricing. Clinicians get the freedom to practice prevention-first medicine, which drives better outcomes and higher satisfaction.

FAQ

Answers for new members.

Is Onevia insurance?

No. Onevia is a membership-based clinic. You can pair membership with a high-deductible plan for major events.

What does membership include?

All primary care visits, preventive care, chronic disease management, telemedicine, and direct access.

How quickly can I be seen?

Members typically receive same- or next-day appointments with extended visit times.

Do you offer employer plans?

Yes. Employer pricing depends on team size and access needs.

Is Pharmacy available now?

Yes. Onevia Rx is live and can be added during enrollment.
