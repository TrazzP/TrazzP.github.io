Our approach

Whole-person care built on relationships.

Onevia is designed to feel different the moment you walk in. We keep panels small so your care team can truly know your story, follow up quickly, and coordinate every detail of your plan.

Your primary physician leads the plan, and our pharmacy, dental, and vision partners stay closely connected so every recommendation feels consistent.

  • Longer visits with the same care team
  • Proactive prevention and wellness planning
  • Shared notes and seamless coordination
  • Transparent membership pricing
Primary Care Physicians

Your trusted medical team

Our physicians lead with empathy, time, and clinical depth. Expect thoughtful visits, proactive wellness plans, and a direct relationship with the people guiding your care.

  • Same- or next-day access
  • Preventive care and chronic condition planning
  • Coordinated referrals when needed
  • Clear, actionable next steps
Pharmacists

Medication experts on your side

Our pharmacy team works directly with your physician to simplify medications, eliminate unnecessary costs, and keep your treatment plan clear.

  • Medication optimization and coaching
  • Refill coordination with your care team
  • At-cost pricing transparency
  • Support for chronic condition care
Dental Clinicians

Preventive-first dentistry

Our dental team focuses on prevention, early detection, and long-term oral health. Members experience a calm, transparent approach without surprise bills.

  • Routine cleanings and exams
  • Clear restorative options
  • Coordinated care with primary team
  • Comfort-forward visits
Optometrists

Vision care that looks ahead

Our optometry partners focus on early detection and long-term eye health. We coordinate findings with your primary team for a complete view of your health.

  • Comprehensive eye health reviews
  • Preventive screenings and monitoring
  • Collaborative care planning
  • Clear recommendations and follow-ups
