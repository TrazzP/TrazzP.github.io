Meet your Specialists!

The heart of Onevia is the people who care for you. Get to know the physicians, pharmacists, dentist, and optometrists who will guide your health journey.

Physicians

Trazz Pepper, MD

Trazz leads Onevia with a vision of healthcare centered on prevention, trust, and long-term relationships. He believes the best outcomes come from truly knowing patients and works to ensure every member feels heard, supported, and empowered in their care.

  • University of Washington School of Medicine (MD)
  • Family Medicine residency, Swedish Medical Center
  • Focus on preventive care and metabolic health
  • Skiing, travel, and trying new recipes

"I want every patient to feel listened to, supported, and cared for."

Pharmacists

Sammy Joe, PharmD

Avery is a medication expert who loves helping people understand their medications. A self-described drug nerd, she's deeply curious about how medications work, how they interact, and how to use them safely and effectively in real life.

  • University of Montana Skaggs School of Pharmacy (PharmD)
  • PGY1 Pharmacy Residency, Providence St. Patrick Hospital
  • Chronic condition support and medication coaching
  • Sunshine, floating rivers, and reading

"I want you to feel confident and informed about every medication you take."

Dentist

  • University of Michigan School of Dentistry (DDS)
