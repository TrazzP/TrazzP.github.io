About Onevia

Meet your Onevia specialists.

The heart of Onevia is the people who care for you. Get to know the physicians, pharmacists, dental clinicians, and optometrists who will guide your membership.

Physicians

Dr. Morgan Lee, MD

Dr. Lee leads the Onevia care team with a focus on prevention, lifestyle medicine, and relationship-first care. She loves building long-term partnerships with members and helping them feel confident about their health plan.

  • Medical school: University of Washington School of Medicine
  • Residency: Family Medicine, Swedish Medical Center
  • Focus: Preventive care, metabolic health, and chronic disease management
  • Hobbies: Trail running, cooking, and travel photography

"I want every member to feel listened to and cared for like family."

Pharmacists

Dr. Avery Chen, PharmD

Dr. Chen specializes in medication optimization and works directly with Onevia physicians to keep your treatment plan simple, affordable, and effective.

  • Pharmacy school: University of Colorado Skaggs School of Pharmacy
  • Residency: Ambulatory Care Pharmacy, Kaiser Permanente
  • Focus: Chronic condition support and medication coaching
  • Hobbies: Gardening, jazz piano, and cycling

"My goal is to make your medications feel easy to understand and easy to manage."

Dentist

Dr. Sofia Ramirez, DDS

Dr. Ramirez brings a gentle, preventive-first approach to dentistry. She focuses on patient comfort, long-term oral health, and clear explanations of every recommendation.

  • Dental school: University of Michigan School of Dentistry
  • Special training: Preventive and restorative dentistry
  • Focus: Gum health, restorative planning, and patient education
  • Hobbies: Hiking with family, ceramics, and community volunteering

"My favorite visits are the ones where patients feel calm and in control."

Optometrists

Dr. Jordan Patel, OD

Dr. Patel focuses on early detection and long-term eye health. He coordinates closely with the Onevia team so vision care fits seamlessly into your overall wellness plan.

  • Optometry school: Pacific University College of Optometry
  • Special training: Ocular disease and preventive screenings
  • Focus: Vision health, early detection, and digital eye strain
  • Hobbies: Snowboarding, reading, and landscape photography

"Eye health is a window into overall health, and I love helping members stay ahead."
